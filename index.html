Before you visit

Frequently asked questions.

Does submitting the form confirm a reservation?

No. The form creates only a booking request. Availability, final pricing, policies, and payment instructions must be confirmed directly by Calilayan Cove.

Are the displayed accommodations and activities final?

The names are based on the current project handover. The resort must still confirm the official list, capacities, inclusions, schedules, and rates.

Can guests arrange events or team-building activities?

The resort has event and activity offerings, but package details, capacity, and availability must be discussed directly with the resort.

Where should guests ask for current prices?

Use the resort’s official Facebook page or listed business email until the production website and booking system are officially approved.
